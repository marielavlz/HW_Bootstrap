About Me

Hello! My name is Mariela Velez. People call me "Mari" or "Mary" for short. I was born in Mayaguez, Puerto Rico and grew up in the state of Texas. I attended high school at Tomball High School and then graduated from The University of Texas at Austin with a major in Microbiology and Infectious Disease and a minor in Jewish Studies.

Some of my favourite things to do is read, write short stories, binge watch shows on Netflix and Hulu, collect Disney animated films, shop online, and play with my niece and nephew. I'm a fan of Game of Thrones/A Song of Ice and Fire, the Harry Potter franchise, the Marvel cinematic universe, and Lord of the Rings as well as other works by J.R.R. Tolkien such as The Hobbit and The Silmarillion.

Finally, some interesting things about me is that I'm fully bilingual, I worked in a bacteriology lab for a year and a half studying P. aeruginosa, and have identified a variety of pathogenic bacteria in a clinical setting. My current plans include becoming a web developer and eventually returning to microbiology to pursue an advanced degree. If you want to know more about me, feel free to approach me and we can talk!
