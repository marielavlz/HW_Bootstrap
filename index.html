About Me

Hello! My name is Mariela Velez Natal, or Mariela Velez for short. People call me "Mari" or "Mary" for convenience since my name is difficult to pronounce in English. I was born in Mayaguez, Puerto Rico and raised in Aguadilla, Puerto Rico. When I was 12 years old, I moved from Puerto Rico to mainland United States where I attended high school at Tomball High School and then graduated from The University of Texas at Austin. I majored in Microbiology and Infectious Disease.

Some of my favourite things to do is read comic books, write fictional short stories, collect Disney animated films, and play with all my little ones. I'm a pround auntie/titi of 3 babies and cousin of a toddler. They're the most important people in my life and I can talk about them for hours.

Finally, some interesting things about me is that I'm fully bilingual, I worked in a bacteriology lab for a year and a half studying P. aeruginosa and loved it, and have identified a variety of pathogenic bacteria before in a clinical setting. I'm not as skilled handling and isolating viruses, but I'm a quick and dedicated learner, so perhaps one day in the future I'll return to that.
