About Me

Hello! My name is Mariela Velez. People call me "Mari" or "Mary" for short. I was born in Mayaguez, Puerto Rico and grew up in the state of Texas. I attended high school at Tomball High School and then graduated from The University of Texas at Austin with a major in Microbiology and Infectious Disease and a minor in Jewish Studies.

I'm a full stack developer with a concentrated interest in front end web development. I'm proficient in JavaScript, CSS3, HTML5, WordPress, and JQuery. I have experiencing developing apps that run on the command line terminal with Node.js and have used Node packages such as Express, Sequelize, SQL and others. I can create and manage databases such as MySQL, and I also have experience in biostastistics using Excel and R Studio.

Finally, some interesting things about me is that I'm fully bilingual, I worked in a bacteriology lab for a year and a half studying P. aeruginosa, and have identified a variety of pathogenic bacteria in a clinical setting. If you want to know more about me, feel free to approach me and we can talk!
